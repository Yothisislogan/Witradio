# WIT Radio Monster Branding

## Goal

Blend the We Insure Things monster branding into the station identity without making the station feel childish.

## Recommended Usage

- neon monster mascot for Dance Party
- sleepy monster artwork for Night Shift
- coffee mug monster for Morning Acoustic
- sunglasses/workday monster for Day Shift

## Audio Branding Ideas

- short monster laugh stingers
- monster-themed station IDs
- "Monster Minute" comedy segment
- playful insurance myth-busting

## Visual Direction

- blue neon accents (#00AEEF)
- synthwave-inspired gradients
- animated equalizers
- retro radio styling
- subtle arcade inspiration

## Avoid

- overly cartoonish sound effects
- excessive memes
- turning the station into a kids station

## Goal Feel

A real internet radio station with a fun personality and memorable branding.
